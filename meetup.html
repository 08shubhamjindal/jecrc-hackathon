ABLE- Meet Ups

Nagpur has an extremely limited number of public places which are easily accessible by wheelchair. Sometimes, this could be one of the limiting factors why wheelchair users in the city don't come out from their homes. This group is for everyone who uses a wheelchair, and would like to connect with similar people to share their experiences, discuss tips on day-to-day living activities, motivate each other and last but not the least come out and have fun with people who share similar situations.

In Chennai Understanding a Disability is by not how you can get in other person's shoes but by understanding their real life problems and taking a step to create awareness in society. Anyone who is proud owner of 'understanding heart and logical brain' wanting to make a change in the world we see today to a brighter future where inclusion is everywhere are welcome here..
